- **Feature: `Final` and `.d.ts` `readonly` immutability is enforced**: `jac check` now rejects writes to immutable bindings with a new `E1121` diagnostic. Reassigning a `typing.Final` local, `has` field, or glob errors at every write form (plain, multi-target, and augmented assignment, tuple unpacking, walrus, `del`), and `readonly` members on structural types synthesized from npm `.d.ts` interfaces are write-protected as well. The declaring assignment, constructor initialization, and one-time `init`/`postinit` assignment of a no-default `Final` field stay legal, matching mypy semantics. (jaseci-labs/jaseci#7354)
